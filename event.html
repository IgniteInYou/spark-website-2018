Fortnite
The wait is over, the game of the year has finally been implemented into a competition. Go head to head with friends(or enemies) to battle it out and finally put an end to the argument - who is the best at Fortnite? The competition will follow a knockout/elimination pattern. The person to stand till the end will be declared the winner. We plan to allow all three platforms: Console, PC and Mobile Devices. We expect you to bring your own PCs and Mobile Phones, but bringing a console is not obligatory.

MCU Quiz
Embodying the true spirit of the ‘Marvel Cinematic Universe’ phenomenon - this championship will examine your understanding of the various aspects of this mythical world. Wallow into a wave of questions, containing your favourite aspects of this fictional universe. “Heroes are made with the path they choose, not the powers they are graced with.” This is the path you must choose to prove yourself as the conquering hero of this championship. Logical Thinking
When you register for this event, you're not just thinking, you’re being logical. The first round of our ‘Logical Thinking event’ will contain a series of questions testing your ability to find practical solutions to a series of challenging problems. Indulge yourself into the world of intuition and critical thinking for a period of 2 hours and work out few of the most exigent problems of the decade. If you’re deemed competent by the wizards of Logic Solving, the finals will assess your genius to the core, for a chance to be crowned as the champion. Participation cost - Rs.100
